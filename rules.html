Katanga Annex Lane One Student Code of Conduct

Official Notice

Misconduct and Corresponding Penalties (Revised)

All residents must obey hall rules. Violation of any rule can attract penalties including fines, disciplinary action, suspension, or loss of residency.

Hall Property and Safety

  1. Cleaning: Students must keep all items and rooms clean. Failure may attract hall discipline and cleaning charges.
  2. Fire extinguisher: Misuse, theft, or damage of extinguishers is prohibited and punishable by the prevailing replacement cost.
  3. Door lock: Where room lock is damaged, the identified student pays for full replacement and key duplication cost.
  4. Louver blades: Damage attracts replacement cost for each affected blade.
  5. Electrical fittings: Damage to sockets, regulators, or fittings attracts full repair cost.
  6. Beds and mattresses: Damage, misuse, or unauthorized movement attracts prevailing replacement or repair cost.
  7. Bath house: Dirtying facilities, misuse of washrooms, or unsanitary behavior is punishable and may lead to eviction.
  8. Plumbing: Blockage caused by misuse attracts repair and restoration charges.
  9. Painting: Graffiti or defacing walls/doors attracts repainting cost and sanctions.
  10. TV rooms: Opening/closing must follow hall authority schedule.

Code of Conduct

  1. No transfer, subletting, or occupation of bed-space by an unauthorized person.
  2. No cooking or use of dangerous appliances in rooms without approval.
  3. No entry/exit outside approved hall hours without permission.
  4. No hosting overnight guests without proper check-in and hall authorization.
  5. No theft, destruction, or tampering with hall property.
  6. No noise disturbance, fights, or conduct that causes public disorder in the hall.
  7. No smoking, vaping, alcohol abuse, narcotics, or possession/use of illegal substances.
  8. No unauthorized photography, recording, or sharing of others' personal details.
  9. No obscene behavior, stealing of room assets, or illegal commercial activities.
  10. Students must act responsibly and comply with JCRC and Hall authority directives.
